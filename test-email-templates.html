Incident Monitoring Email Templates

Available Placeholders:

  • {{user_name}} - Recipient's full name
  • {{monitor_name}} - Monitor name (clickable URL)
  • {{monitor_url}} - URL to monitor details page
  • {{checked_url}} - Full checked URL
  • {{root_cause}} - Root cause of the incident
  • {{incident_start}} - When the incident started
  • {{incident_resolved}} - When the incident was resolved (resolved email only)
  • {{incident_duration}} - Total downtime duration (resolved email only)
  • {{incident_details_url}} - URL to incident details page
  • {{comment_incident_url}} - URL to comment on incident (resolved email only)

Implementation Notes:

  • Both templates are fully responsive and work on all major email clients
  • Replace the logo placeholder with your actual logo URL
  • Update the footer copyright text with your company name
  • The templates use inline CSS for maximum email client compatibility
  • Colors follow the style guidelines: red/orange for down, green for up
  • All placeholders should be replaced server-side before sending
  • The {{monitor_url}}, {{incident_details_url}}, and {{comment_incident_url}} placeholders need to be added to your template engine
